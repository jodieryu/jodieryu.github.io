Hey there! I'm Jodie - a senior product manager, developer, and design enthusiast hoping to further my career, knowledge, and growth in different product spaces.

I was born & raised in Vienna, Virginia. Growing up, I loved drawing comics for my friends and playing online puzzle games. Those joys eventually followed me to college, where I took art classes for fun and found a passion for problem solving through my computer science courses. Imagine my surprise when I discovered Product Management, where I could be creative & analytical!

I strive to help build products that make people's lives better, whether that be in their day-to-day jobs or if they're just passing by as a consumer. I love seeking solutions to complex problems and thrive in the midst of chaos. Among all, teamwork is very important to me, making a successful product can't be done without the collaboration, hard work, and perspective of multiple teams to come up with creative solutions.
