Drive, Chip, and Putt

Drive, Chip, and Putt is a free nationwide junior golf development competition aimed at growing the game by focusing on the three fundamental skills employed in golf.

*Due to a non-disclosure agreement with Brightspot, I am limited in the amount of content I can display.

MY ROLE

I worked with three joint stakeholders to execute the redesign of the frontend facing site and implementation of new features from conception to launch. I conducted requirements gathering, research, buildout of the product roadmap, and operated customer onboarding and training.

My responsiblities extended to working closely with the UX designer and lead engineer. I also provided on-site editorial and product support for the DCP National Finals.

RESULTS

  • Successful site launch & support for a new event management portal and scoring web application.
  • Users are able to efficiently create, manage, and edit local/sub-regional/regional qualifiers in a centeralized system.
  • Event Managers can easily produce and present content for the entire lifecycle of the season, from registration, management of the participant roster, creation of tee times, and finalization of scoring post-event.
  • Scoring data can now be entered on-site during the event rather than post-event.
