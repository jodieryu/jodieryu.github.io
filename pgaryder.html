PGA Championship and Ryder Cup

The PGA Championship is one of the four men's major championships in professional golf and the Ryder Cup is a biennial men's golf competition between teams from Europe and the United States. The branding of the site changes yearly, and the content is updated to reflect the current lifecycle of the tournament (pre-event, live event, post-event).

*Due to a non-disclosure agreement with Brightspot, I am limited in the amount of content I can display.

MY ROLE

I collaborated & led a development team of eight frontend and backend software engineers and five mobile app developers to replatform the stakeholders legacy content management system (CMS) and redesign of the sites frontend and mobile experience. I managed the product as it passed through the various stages of requirements gathering, design, user acceptance testing, and initial MVP.

My responsiblities extended to communicating with more than 15 vendors to incorporate custom integrations around analytics, ads, live video, and live scoring. I also provided on-site editorial and product support for the Ryder Cup and remotely for the Championship sites during the live event.

RESULTS

  • Successful site launch & support for six different golf tournaments.
  • Users are able to efficiently manage hundreds of assets using the built-in digital asset manager, giving them the ability to access and organize a broad set of content.
  • Editors can easily produce and present content for the entire lifecycle of the tournament, from off-season to the live event.
  • Publishing content on the iOS and Android mobile apps no longer requires code deployments and/or app store submissions.
